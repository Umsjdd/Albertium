Experience Design

UX Strategy

01

Narrative Navigation

Users don't browse — they experience. Each page tells a story, guiding visitors through Albertium's world with intentional pacing and rhythm.

02

Visual-First Discovery

Projects are discovered through imagery, not lists. Immersive visuals create emotional connection before a single word is read.

03

Frictionless Conversion

Every page naturally guides toward action. Strategic CTA placement ensures interested visitors always have a clear next step.

Interactive Layer

Motion Design

Parallax Scrolling

Layered depth creates cinematic feel. Hero content moves at different speeds, creating a sense of dimension.

Reveal Animations

Content elegantly enters the viewport. Staggered timing creates rhythm and guides the eye through the page.

Image Transitions

Smooth scale, clip-path reveals, and overlay transitions on project imagery. Every image makes an entrance.

Micro-interactions

Hover states, button animations, cursor effects. Every detail is considered, every interaction feels intentional.
